About me

I am a postdoctoral researcher in the Computer Science and Engineering Department at the University at Buffalo, SUNY where I am being advised by Prof. Haonan Lu. At SUNY-Buffalo, I am a member of the Correct, high-Performance, practical Impact-Network Systems (CPI-NS) research group. Before joining CPI-NS, I received my Ph.D. from the same Department under the supervision of Prof. Murat Demirbas. My research mainly is distributed systems.
