About

I am a doctoral candidate at University at Buffalo, SUNY, Computer Science Department. My research interests lie in the fields of Distributed Systems, Blockchain Systems, and Machine Learning Systems. Before coming to Buffalo, I finished Bachelor of Science in Computer Science from King Khalid University, Abha, Saudi Arabia and a Master of Science in Computer Science from the University of Connecticut, Storrs, CT, United States.
